LOUCA23 is managed collaboratively by local open source communities in Indonesia, including in terms of activity funding. Funding for activities in LOUCA23 comes from 3 sources, namely (a) open donations, (b) sponsorship, and (c) participant registration.

Open Donation

Open donations are a source of funding received through activity donors with a minimum amount of IDR 200,000 up to unlimited. Donors at the LOUCA23 event are not limited to individuals, but also from communities, foundations, companies, and other institutions.

Donations can be given in cash or non-cash, such as financing certain sectors such as consumption, servers, souvenirs, and so on. If you are interested in participating in making LOUCA23 successful by donating, don't hesitate to contact us via the LOUCA23 Official WhatsApp.
